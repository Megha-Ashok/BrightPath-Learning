About the Project

BrightPath Learning is a Machine Learning-based platform designed to predict student performance based on diverse academic and personal parameters. Our mission is to support educational institutions in identifying at-risk students early and to empower learners with customized feedback and growth strategies. Future expansions include emotional analysis using NLP and automated career guidance systems.

Core Features

ML-Based Prediction

Utilizes various algorithms like Linear Regression, Decision Tree, and XGBoost for accurate predictions.

Insightful Dashboard

Graphical representation of student trends, learning gaps, and achievement metrics.

Support System

Recommendations for teachers and counselors to provide targeted support.

Future Scope

Integration with NLP for sentiment analysis and emotional understanding.
