What is BrightPath Learning?

BrightPath Learning is an AI-powered academic support platform that helps predict student performance, provide learning recommendations, and guide them with useful educational tools. Whether you're a student or an educator, BrightPath helps personalize the learning journey and boosts academic success.

Key Features

📈 Student Performance Prediction

Upload academic data and get predicted results using machine learning.

📚 Study Resources

Access curated videos, PDFs, and notes based on your subjects.

📊 Academic Progress Tracker

Visualize progress reports and identify areas to improve.

💬 Mentor & Peer Chat

Connect with your batchmates or mentors for academic help.
